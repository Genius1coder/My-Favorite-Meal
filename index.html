Party Jollof Rice

Food Description

Party jollof rice is a very popular Nigerian food that is typically serve at every party in Nigeria. It has a distinct taste that sets it apart from other types of cooked rice. The quantity of rice and ingredients needed to cook party jollof rice that will serve 5 adults are listed in the table below.

html table

Ingredients Quantity
Rice 4 cups
Onions 2 medium size
Red bell pepper 5 pieces
Tomato paste 240 grams
Scotch bonnet pepper 5 pieces
Ginger 1 piece
Garlic clove 2 pieces
Fresh Tomato 10 pieces
Vegetable oil half cup
Chicken stock 1 cup
Magarine 50 grams
Bay leaves 4 pieces
Curry 1 tablespoon
Thyme 1 tablespoon
Maggi 4 cubes
Salt 2 tablespoon

Cooking Instructions

  1. Set your gas burner to medium heat and place an empty pot on it.
  2. Add vegetable oil to the pot and allow it to heat up for 2 minutes.
  3. Add diced onion, tomato paste and bay leaves to the oil and fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add blended pepper (tomato, scotch bonnet pepper, garlic, clove, onion and bell pepper) to the pot
  5. Allow to cook for 5 minutes and then add chicken stock. Stir the mixture very well.
  6. Add spices (salt, thyme, curry, maggi and margarine). Allow to cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the washed rice and some water. Stir very well to ensure proper mixing.
  8. Cover the pot and cook for 40 minutes.
  9. At the end of 40 minutes use turning stick to turn the rice very well.
  10. Cover the pot and cook for another 5 minutes.
  11. Turn off the heat and turn the rice one more time with turning stick.

Wow! Our party jollof rice is ready!

The food can be served with moin-moin, fried plantain, fried chicken and any chilled drink of choice.
